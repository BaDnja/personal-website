Projects

Glasajmo.ba

The project aims to facilitate voting in elections for Bosnian citizens living outside the country. Its purpose is to encourage more people with Bosnian passports to participate in the voting process. The backend of the application is developed using Python, Django, and a PostgreSQL database. The entire system is hosted on a VPS (Virtual Private Server) for optimal performance and security.

The project provides a user registration feature, allowing eligible citizens to sign up and associate themselves with a representative who will assist them throughout the voting process. Additionally, the application includes an admin panel that empowers the associates to monitor and manage registrations based on the voter's respective country of residence. This feature streamlines the voting procedure, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience for all participants.
